Koeksisters Recipe

This traditional South African delicacy forever remains a local favorite as one bite of this pudding summons fond memories from childhood for many in the region. When sailing on one of our Africa Safari and Wildlife cruises, our expert chefs prepare this scrumptious treat on board.

Ingredients

  • 500 g sugar
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/4 tsp ginger ground
  • 1 cup rooibos strong tea
  • lemon halved, juice only

Preperation

  1. Heat the sugar, spices, tea and lemon juice until dissolved.
  2. Sieve flour and rub butter in with fingertips until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  3. Make a well in the centre and add enough milk to form a soft dough.
  4. AKnead gently until smooth. Roll out thinly, cut strips and plait to form small koeksisters.
