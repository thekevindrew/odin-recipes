Malva Pudding Recipe

This traditional South African delicacy forever remains a local favorite as one bite of this pudding summons fond memories from childhood for many in the region. When sailing on one of our Africa Safari and Wildlife cruises, our expert chefs prepare this scrumptious treat on board.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup sugar (180g)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon apricot jam
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Preperation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a small oven dish (7 x 7 x 1 ½ inch Pyrex dish).
  2. Beat or whip the sugar and eggs, until thick and lemon coloured.
  3. Melt the butter (don't boil) and add the butter and vinegar to the wet mixture.
  4. Add this mixture with the milk to the egg mixture in the processor or mixing bowl.
